Why is Color Important?

  • Adequate color control ensures that content is understand and accessible for all individuals with visual impairments like color blindness or low vision, as well as improving readability for everyone

  • Color Standards ensures legal compliance for sites that have government ties as well, preventing exclusionary design

  • Forgetting about how good color constrast meets accessibility standards, legible color constrast improves the users quality of life, minimizing eye strain and overall usability/readibility
