A Site to Learn the Basics of Universal Design!

Why Universal Design?

  • Universal Design has been and will always be a growing branch of development. It promotes inclusivity, accesibility and usability for such a wide range of users

  • Keeping up with Universal Design allows for not only a fully functional webpage, but an easily accessible page for all types of people whether they may be held back by disabilities

  • Disabilities are much more prevalent all across the world than some might expect, with about 15% of most populations sturggling with some sort of impairment. Building around Universal Design first instead of last allows developers to create a much greater workign website as well as easier to build upon in the future.

What is this site?

  • To show the importance of some of these Universal Design aspects, I created 3 different pages to showcase how it can improve user experience

  • Tab Targetting Showcase one of the features that is important among those unable to use a mouse fully

  • Color Contrasting Present extreme examples of how consideration of contrasting is important for those with vision impairment
